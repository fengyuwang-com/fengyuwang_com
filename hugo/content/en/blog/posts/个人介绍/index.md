My work is built on a core belief: every complex problem can be broken down through a clear logical framework, analysed efficiently with the help of artificial intelligence, and turned into systematic solutions that are reusable, replicable, and actionable.

I offer decision planning and problem-solving services to individuals, entrepreneurs, and businesses, helping clients establish clear judgement in complex environments, reduce risk, optimise pathways, improve efficiency, and achieve long-term goals.

My service areas cover business development, marketing operations, media and communications, software applications, career development, life planning, and resource allocation.

I am skilled at distinguishing genuine technological innovation from marketing hype, identifying market competition and business risks, and helping clients avoid traps and reduce costly trial and error.

Drawing on systematic analytical frameworks and a broad perspective, combined with AI tools, I integrate trend analysis, historical patterns, and practical logic to deliver executable and verifiable solutions.

I also create content guided by the principle of enhancing human wellbeing, providing valuable information and positive life experiences to society. For inquiries, please contact me at w@fengyu.hk. Fees are charged only after a solution has been proposed and agreed upon.
