Empty Space & Essence

Chinese landscape painting is not about piling on detail. A thousand miles of river and mountain are rendered not by filling the canvas, but by leaving room for the imagination. Picasso's trajectory mirrored the same truth in reverse—he started with photographic realism and spent a lifetime reducing it to a few lines. Two roads, same destination. Good abstraction does not mean removing detail. It means keeping only the details that matter.
